ive been

i am

too much

i am just too much 

there is so much

there is so much

such abundance

how am i to sleep

forgive me o thee!

dear sleep

miss me while i’m away

and call upon my name only when the sound of your voice will cradle me in blue and
purple space

cold blue ethereal night sky

cradle me o sleep!

along with everyone i join!

all my fellow fellows!

allow me to follow

to wake and sleep seamlessly

not name my fellows across the hoard

o sleep days are so rejoiceful

days are so rejoiceful

what will you bring?
