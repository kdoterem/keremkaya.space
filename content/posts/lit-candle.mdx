lit candle 
slit my wrist
make these believers
forget what they believe
make these doubters
learn what they doubt
make them black out and
bawl out their eyes
gouge their eyes as you wait for me
oh, lit candle
i cannot see
spring never sprung
king never crowned
killer’s body swung
and i swallowed my tongue
