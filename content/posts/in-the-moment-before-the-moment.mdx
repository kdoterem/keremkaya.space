you’re not defeated until you’re beaten

your greatest shame

what’s your greatest shame?

is it appearing stupid or making someone feel stupid

does it stem from a place of humbleness or pride

how confident are you in your inconfidence

what you got when you got nothing

ain’t that the toughest predicament
