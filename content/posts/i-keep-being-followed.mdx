if you’re reading this that means i keep being followed
when i wrote this i was being followed
so if you’re reading this that means i’m still followed
though hated,
but still followed
i stopped walking
they stopped walking too
i stopped talking
they stopped talking too
i stop they stop
i move they move
is this all you can muster what they say to me
as if it would make a difference to me
i say they say
nothing keeps them at bay
