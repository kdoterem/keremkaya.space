i don’t shoot down anybody’s ideas

i never not engage

never not give my %100

im sorry my %100 really sucks when i have it

im looking for places to give it

and im sorry for always trying to give it all

when it requires less

and im sorry that makes me really weird

it’s just

i don’t really like it when it’s all with me

these hands are conspiring against me to tear down what i don’t like

then i have to face that im weird and im sorry for being weird

these hands then build me back up

and now i have %200 to give

cause it really is going to hurt

when i see these hands

but either way im weird

and even more

sorry

can you tell me some nice things please
