a 128 piece puzzle can be quite fun

you have all the big pieces and the big gaps

everything attaches very smoothly

my coffee is rather bitter this morning

but i have the sun

hitting my hardened face from the winter

i made extra coffee for my sister

whose alarm is keep ringing and i find myself contemplating on whether i should wake
her up with a cup or

let her be as her alarm lightens her sleep to the point where she’s ready to get up

it’s been 2 summers i spent orangeless

this morning

this winter morning

fully clothed in black

as my mug and my coffee

it was orange

largest puzzle is called what a wonderful world

at 60.000 pieces

and i have 300.000 loves
