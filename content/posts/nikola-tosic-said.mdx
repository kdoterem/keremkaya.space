nikola tosic said

i have to again
apologize
for how shitty and cringe
my poems are.
sometimes i read them
and i am
utterly mortified
how shitty they are.
including this one.

i have been saying it too

he also said no poems on a saturday

wonder what he does on a saturday

laugh on a saturday

make love on a saturday

wouldn’t saturday be the ideal time to write poems

to wind down and reflect

assuming he lives in the circadian rhthym of industrialization working clock

that doesn’t matter though

does taking his work and stamping on them make me an artist
