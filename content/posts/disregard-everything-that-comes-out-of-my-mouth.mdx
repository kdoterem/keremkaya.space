or spills out of my fingers

sweat out of my skin

poetry is not it anymore

poetry is not written

it's lived and i live pretty poetically

me and the universe know

waters that open to let me in

skies that erupt

what am i even saying

written poetry is for those with things to say with no way to say it

and i guess i have a way to say them now

goodbye
